Our primary focus is on advancing the study and application of scientometrics, which involves the quantitative analysis of scientific literature, research trends, and the impact of scientific activities.

We welcome scholars, researchers, professionals, and students from diverse fields who are interested in scientometrics, bibliometrics, and research evaluation. Importantly, these should be affiliated with Israeli academic institutes.

We organize workshops, seminars, conferences, and collaborative research projects aimed at promoting knowledge exchange, methodological advancements, and networking among members.

As a member, you gain access to a network of experts, cutting-edge research, opportunities for collaboration, and resources that can enhance your understanding and application of scientometric methods.
About Us

The Israeli Scientometrics Academic Community

Welcome to the Israeli Scientometrics Academic Community, where we bring together scholars and professionals dedicated to advancing the study of scientometrics in Israel. Our mission is to foster collaboration, promote research excellence, and provide valuable insights into the dynamics of scientific research and innovation. Join us as we explore the impact of scientific activities through rigorous analysis and data-driven approaches.

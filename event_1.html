The first community gathering

Dear Colleagues,

We are delighted to invite you to the inaugural gathering of the Israeli Academic Scientometrics Community. This event marks a significant milestone in fostering collaboration and knowledge-sharing among researchers dedicated to the Science of Science (SoS) and scientometrics.

Location: Bar Ilan University
Date: 21.11.2024
Duration: 11:30 - 14:00

Agenda:

  • 11:30-12:00 - Food and gathering.
  • 12:00-12:30 - Guest lecture by Prof. Lutz Bronmann from Max Planck Society about "Investigating the relationship of science and society based on scientometric data".
  • 12:30-13:00 - Discussions on several important manner, including head of the organization, domain name, etc.
  • 13:00-14:00 - Social time and further subjects from the ISAC members.

A more detailed agenda will be sent in the monthly newsletter.

Please confirm your attendance using the following link. Your prompt response will help us in making necessary arrangements for the event.

We look forward to your participation and are excited about the potential collaborations and advancements that will emerge from our collective efforts. Let us come together to share our insights, build lasting connections, and pave the way for the future of scientometrics research in Israel.
